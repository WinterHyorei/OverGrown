OverGrown

When humanity’s hunger for control eats itself.




ABOUT

The year, 5864. The World is Plagued by 7 pandemics at once. In response humanity confined themselves within 63 Megawalls constructed around the world 13 of which encompassing important resources. 6 Battle Maidens are women from each pandemic plagued settlements, not only surving but thriving enough to master their predicament. 1 man with his Ai Companion. Treck the infested world with a mission to ensure humanity Survives the inevitable Collapse of the very walls humanity called home.




ORIGIN

A mysterious pandemic caused the death of about one-fourth of humanity. United, the remaining global superpowers and conglomerates created a new governing body with an emphasis on controlling what’s left of civilization by constructing Megawalls to contain and preserve humanity’s survival.

Due to dwindling resources, the new governing body — named the Global Wall Coalition — established “The 13 Megawalls Project.” These structures, built around thirteen global locations, were massive enough to encompass entire countries, each chosen for their unique resources. The project’s success led to over fifty smaller walls, constructed by private sectors, enclosing city-states scattered across the world.

To combat the decline of natural resources like Plant Life, Accessible Food, Knowledge, Confidence, and Physical Adaptability, the Coalition funded companies to bioengineer Artificial Solutions — with the goal of creating sustainable life within the walls.



UNTIL IT WASNT



7 PANDEMICS

Plant life bioengineered to spread no matter what. Made to take over the barren world of OverGrown, after the inevitable plant life exinction. Staying true to its roots, it now grows uncontrollably outgrowing its surroundings causing massive infrastructure damage and hosting wildlife to the point of over population.

Hungry for cacncer meat? world hunger tripled and humanity needed a substitute for meat, developing canned processed food out of multiplying cacncer injected into animals. humanity’s gluttony overconsuming cancer meat causes assimilation mutating them into ever spreading blob of flesh and tissue.

Automation was one of the most successful progressions humanity accomplished. Ai integrated into daily life it was inevitable, humans abused the system to the point it now hungers for flesh. fusing with the flesh hijacking the brain, it now uses the human body as a vessel to spread.

Once a drug to induce Artificial Confidence Boost mutated into paranoia, capgras syndrome, and tribalism. The ones infected with the PrimalWail loses all morality and guidance replaced by the 3 primal desires being MURDER, LUST, and GLUTTONY which varries per person. They now live in isolation hunting down those uninfected by the Wail, seeing them as nothing more than prey.

A shortcut to human evolution. Humanities artificial solution to forced adaptability, once used to amplify the work force and military. The mutation turned against humanity after gaining consciousness. Subjecting its users into forced evolution through mutaion, as it warps the users body into monstrous forms. Feeding on white blood cells in the blood to act as pain killer to the constant pain and agony.

A psychological collapse of the collective human consciousness. What once was an artificial solution to Brainrot turned into unnatural intellect boost through exposure to sunlight. Victims become highly capable thinkers, strategists, tacticians, and oracles inexchange of freedom from the light as over exposure to sunlight causes severe suicidal tendencies.
